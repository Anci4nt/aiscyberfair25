Haywood Farms

This luxurious estate of over 4.5 acres is what a farm needs- Trees, animals and the charm of grass's greenness. Buffalos, cows, rabbits, geese, roosters and hens; They have them all. You can watch, feed and cuddle these cute animals. The gentle wind blowing through the plants make you want to stay there all day. They have strawberries, radishes, oranges, carrots, peas, fenugreek and coriander to name but a few. You will experience the joy and contentment of harvesting these vegetables and have a taste of India's village rural way of life. They have cozy appealing sitting places and their food is delicious and mouthwatering. All that is in the farm is organic; grown from organic manures, neem oil and a special manure by the name jeevaamrit. The strawberries are rich red in color with ravishing looks which compel you to eat more and more. All the fruits and vegetables are so sweet and juicy; the best thing is that you can carry some of them back home. Watermelons, muskmelons, jack fruits and avocados will be in the limelight in the coming season as grown these days, now very popular strawberries. The farm can hold 150 people and is used for birthday parties and events. The whole business is family run where your interests come first. The farm is an ideal combination of maximised entrepreneurship and business savvy.
